DISCOVER OUR STORY


Home of the Famous Pancit Malabon







Amber's Philippines is a renowned culinary destination known for its rich heritage in Filipino cuisine. Established in 1988, we have been delighting taste buds for over three decades with our authentic flavors and warm hospitality. Our tagline, "Home of the Famous Pancit Malabon," embodies our dedication to showcasing beloved Filipino dishes prepared with the finest ingredients and traditional recipes.

Join us on a culinary journey filled with nostalgia, delicious memories, and the vibrant flavors of the Philippines. Whether you're craving classic favorites or exploring new culinary adventures, Amber's Philippines welcomes you to savor the true taste of Filipino cuisine.
